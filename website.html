MAL members are invited to send in their questions or comments for the AX panel guests through the pre-event survey below. 10 lucky winners will be selected from among entrants to receive either a $20 Amazon Gift Card or a limited AX attendee bonus – an illustrated shikishi (printed).About Sparks of TomorrowAiring on July 5, 2026! Steampunk meets historical Kyoto in the next masterfully animated TV series from beloved studio Kyoto Animation. With stunning background art reminiscent of Impressionist paintings and intricate animation, Sparks of Tomorrow will have production quality equal to KyoAni fan-favorites such as Violet Evergarden! Set in an alternative version of the early 20th-century—where steam power has ground industrial development to a halt—Kyoto is shrouded in soot, smoke, and steam. Kihachi, a skeptical boy, is haunted by the loss of his brother with whom he once dreamed of an "Age of Electricity." Inako, a deeply spiritual girl, feels forced to hide both her dreams and her regrets for her late mother. Together, they uncover the secrets of the “20th Century Electrical Catalog”, and reclaim their dreams in this story of regeneration.
